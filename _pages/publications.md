### Hybrid attention-based Swin Transformer super-resolution reconstruction of turbulent flow  
![Paper Thumbnail](/assets/images/2.png)  
*Physics of Fluids*, 36(6), 065132 (2024)

**Authors:** Li Xin, et al.  

This work proposes a hybrid attention-based Swin Transformer framework for super-resolution reconstruction of turbulent flow fields. The model integrates spatial attention and channel attention mechanisms to improve reconstruction fidelity and capture multi-scale flow structures effectively.
